Common questions

Frequently asked.

Do you ship internationally?
Yes — tracked, insured delivery to every country we can reach. Most parcels arrive within 2–7 working days.
Can I order a signed copy?
Signed editions are listed individually in the shop. If we have a signing scheduled for a title you want, write to us and we'll reserve a copy.
Do you offer library & institutional accounts?
We do — universities, libraries and design studios. Write to hello@inkwellspine.com for an account application.
Can you source a rare or out-of-print book?
Often. Our rare books desk searches international stock weekly. Send the title, author and any edition details you have.
What is your returns policy?
Unread books can be returned within 30 days for a full refund. We cover return postage within the EU.

Prefer to talk in person?

Drop in any day except Monday. Coffee is always on.
